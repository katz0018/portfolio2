PROCESS:

We forget to laugh at the little things and sometimes we let stress take over. We're all guilty of this, and it's okay! With International Laugh Day coming around the corner, I wanted to build a mini public service announcement that brings awareness to the positive impact of laughing and how contagious it can be. Honestly, I just wanted to grab the attention of others and cause a little giggle to improve their day.



AUDIENCE:

This mini announcement is aimed towards full-time working individuals or students. Individuals that spend a good amount of time using social media or browsing the internet.



RATIONALE:

When envisioning this piece, I felt my audience could relate to the 90's Retro feel of a video. It would reminiscent of public service announcements like House Hippo. I achieved the 90's look by adding an off-white background, incorporating rotoscoping and using a font from those years. I didn't add music so the laughter would stand out even more while scrolling on social media feeds. The different laughing mouths represent diversity and the idea that regardless of your background, laughing is contagious.
